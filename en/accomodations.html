Accommodations
Our Chambre d'hôtes has 5 rooms available, 2 double rooms and 3 family rooms. Each with a bathroom and toilet. Our rooms have names that we found appropriate to the environment and experience here in the beautiful Indre.

Le relax

A double room with 2x1-bed and a private bathroom and toilet. Ideal to relax in!

Rich breakfast

Wifi

Towels and bed linen

No smoking

1 extra bed possible
(+€30/night)

1 extra babybed possible
(+€10/night)

€80 /night

€70 in low season

If you book for 1 night a surcharge of €30 is applicable.

Le soleil & La lune

These are 2 rooms for a family (or friends) with respectively a double bed and 3 single beds. The rooms share the bathroom and toilet in the hallway, which can only be used by them. Le soleil can also be booked separately and then has the bathroom and toilet in the hallway for himself.

Rich breakfast

Wifi

Towels and bed linen

No smoking

1 extra bed possible
(+€30/night)

1 extra babybed possible
(+€10/night)

€150 /night

€130 in low season

If Le soleil is booked without La lune the prices are the same as those for Le relax and Le voyage

If you book for 1 night a surcharge of €60 is applicable.

L'aventure

A room with 2 single beds and 1 bunk bed (for children up to 12 years) Ideal for a great adventure!

Our beautiful chalet for 4 to 6 people has 1 bedroom with double bed and a private bathroom + 1 room with 2 single beds and a sleeping couch for max 2 persons.
