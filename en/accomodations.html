Accomodations
Our Chambre d'hôtes has 5 rooms available, 2 double rooms and 3 family rooms. Each with a bathroom and toilet. Our rooms have names that we found appropriate to the environment and experience here in the beautiful Indre.

Le relax

A double room with 2x1-bed and a private bathroom and toilet. Ideal to relax in!

Rich breakfast

Wifi

Towels and bed linen

No smoking

1 extra bed possible
(+€30/night)

No pets allowed

1 extra babybed possible
(+€10/night)

€80 /night (July - Aug)

€70 in low season (Sept - June)

If you book for 1 night a surcharge of €30 is applicable..

Le soleil & La lune

These are 2 rooms for a family (or friends) with respectively a double bed and 3 single beds. The rooms share the bathroom and toilet in the hallway, which can only be used by them. Le soleil can also be booked separately and then has the bathroom and toilet in the hallway for itself.

Rich breakfast

Wifi

Towels and bed linen

No smoking

1 extra bed possible pour la lune
(+€30/night)

No pets allowed

1 extra babybed possible for le soleil
(+€10/night)

€150 /night (July - Aug) for both rooms (= 4 people)

€130 in low season (Sept - June)

If Le soleil is booked without La lune the prices are the same as those for Le relax and Le voyage

If you book for 1 night a surcharge of €30 is applicable.

La nature

Our beautiful chalet for 4 to 6 people has 1 bedroom with double bed and a private bathroom + 1 room with 2 single beds and a sleeping couch for max 2 persons.

Rich breakfast

Wifi

Towels and bed linen

There are also arrangements possible if you would like to rent the chambres d'hôtes for a big family trip, a party, for a teambuilding with your employees or for various sports activities with your sports club. The arrangements will be tuned on your needs.
