Activities
On our property but also in the surrounding area there are a range of activities to experience. You can challenge your friends and family for a game of darts, viking kubb, ping-pong or jeux de boules. You can also go hiking or biking in a beautiful scenery, make a splash in the pool, visit a castle or a cozy town, go to an adventure park and much more!
Cycling
The region lends itself to beautiful bicycle tours which we can map out for you if desired. We also offer guided cycling tours with the racing bike or mtb. Arrangements for this are on request and will be worked out according to your personal wishes.
Animals
On our domain you can have fun with different animals. We have our 4 goats, 2 cats, 2 dogs, 15 chickens and 3 cute donkeys. On request there are even hikes possible with the donkeys!
Bar
On our domain there is also a cozy bar where you can have a snack at noon or enjoy a nice beverage in the evening!
Fitness
On request you can use the fitness equipment we have available.
Swimming
Enjoy a refreshing dive in our swimming pool. Or stretch out on our sun loungers!
Hiking
Enjoy a foggy morning with a beautiful sunrise on your morning walk or go any moment for a casual walk in the beautiful outdoors.

In the area

Near our Maison there are countless other adventures to experience. There are several cozy towns with culture and shopping opportunities as well as markets and flea markets to roam around. Then there are abbeys and castles surrounded by vineyards to visit. Furthermore you can enjoy yourself on the golf course, in a kayak or in the adventure park which even has a beach! You can also relax in the various nature parks or go fishing in one of the many lakes and rivers.
