Activities
On our property but also in the surrounding area there are a range of activities to experience. You can challenge your friends and family for a game of darts, viking kubb, ping-pong or jeux de boules. You can also go hiking or biking in a beautiful scenery, make a splash in the pool, visit a castle or a cozy town, go to an adventure park and much more! We have already prepared various day trips and are also happy to assist you with advice and recommendations.
Cycling
TThe region is perfect for beautiful cycling routes (15-100 km) that we have already planned and can be downloaded on Strava. Upon request, we can also create larger cycling routes.
Animals
On our property, we have goats, cats, dogs, chickens, and donkeys. Every morning after breakfast, children (and parents) can join in feeding and cuddling these animals.
Bistro
On the property, there's also a cozy bistro where you can enjoy a delicious cocktail, wine, beer, or a refreshing drink.
Horse riding
You can bring your own horse on your vacation. We have a very beautiful outdoor arena and a pasture with a shelter. We are located in the middle of nature, making it ideal for beautiful outdoor rides.
Swimming
Enjoy a refreshing dip in our swimming pool or stretch out on one of our lounge chairs and relax during your vacation.
Hiking
Hiking enthusiasts can indulge their passion here. We have trails ranging from 2.5 to 20 km mapped out starting from our B&B or in the vicinity, which you can easily download.

In the area

Near our Maison there are countless other adventures to experience. There are several cozy towns with culture and shopping opportunities as well as markets and flea markets to roam around. Then there are abbeys and castles surrounded by vineyards to visit. Furthermore you can enjoy yourself on the golf course, in a kayak or in the adventure park which even has a beach! You can also relax in the various nature parks or go fishing in one of the many lakes and rivers.
